# Exercise 1 - thumb -
- fret a C major chord
```jtab
<div class="jtab chordonly"> C </div>
```
- Then use your thumb to pluck the bass notes, alternating between the A and D strings:
```jtab
$5 3 $4 2 $5 3 $4 2 | $5 3 $4 2 $5 3 $4 2 ||
```
***
# Exercise 2 - thumb and middle finger -
- fret a C major chord
- Two-finger technique, you’ll use your **middle finger** to play the B in between bass notes.
```jtab
$5 3 $2 1 $4 2 $2 1 | $5 3 $2 1 $4 2 $2 1 ||
```
***
# Exercise 3 - thumb, middle finger and index finger -
- Three fingers in the mix. Your index finger will play the G string.
```jtab
$5 3 $2 1 $4 2 $3 0 | $5 3 $2 1 $4 2 $3 0 ||
```
